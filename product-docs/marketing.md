# Keto Tracker - Marketing Material

**Target Audience**: People following Dr. Boz's ketogenic diet continuum

---

## Elevator Pitch (30 Seconds)

"Keto Tracker simplifies tracking your progress through Dr. Boz's 12-phase ketogenic diet. Log daily glucose and ketones, automatically calculate your Dr. Boz Ratio, and see your progress through each phase with clear visual feedback. All data stays on your device for complete privacy."

---

## Key Benefits

### 1. **No Manual Math**
Enter glucose and ketones, get your Dr. Boz Ratio instantly. Clear color-coded feedback shows how you're doing.

### 2. **Automatic Phase Tracking**
See where you are in the 12-phase continuum, what's next, and how long until advancement. No guessing.

### 3. **Visual Progress**
7-day charts show your ratio trends. See improvements over time with clear visual feedback.

### 4. **Complete Privacy**
All data stored locally on your device. No cloud sync required, complete control over your health data.

### 5. **Quick Daily Use**
Log metrics in seconds. Simple interface designed for daily tracking without hassle.
